# Logo Setup

Place your logo and illustration files in this directory with the following names:

1. **dialog.png** - Dialog logo
2. **entc.png** - ENTC logo  
3. **partner.png** - Partner/Sponsor logo
4. **steps.png** - VR Cycling illustration/steps diagram

## Recommended Specifications

### Logos (dialog.png, entc.png, partner.png)
- **Format**: PNG with transparent background
- **Dimensions**: Approximately 200x80 pixels (will be auto-scaled)
- **File size**: Keep under 100KB for optimal loading
- **Display**: White tiles at bottom of left panel
- **Height**: Auto-scaled to fit within 35px

### Illustration (steps.png)
- **Format**: PNG or JPG
- **Dimensions**: Approximately 600x200 pixels (landscape)
- **File size**: Keep under 500KB for optimal loading
- **Display**: Above logos section with rounded corners
- **Height**: Maximum 152px

## Notes

- All files will be hidden automatically if missing (no broken images)
- The illustration appears above the logo tiles
- Maintain aspect ratios for best appearance
